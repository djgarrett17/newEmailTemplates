Do you feel at home on the beachside? Check out these exceptional beaches in 2023

Navagio Beach, Greece

If you want one of the most picturesque spots in the world Navagio Beach located in Zakynthos, Greece is for you. This pebble beach is wedged between towering cliffs with an Instagram worthy shipwreck on land for the perfect photo back drop.

Nyang Nyang Beach, Indonesia

Bali is already a great beach location, however, if you want one of the most pristine beaches in the world Nyang Nyang is a remote, secluded, white sand beach like no other. If you are up for adventure and tracking down a cliffside to reach a remote location this is a beach for you.

Bazaruto Island, Mozambique

Located in Bazaruto National Park, this island offers beach lovers tons of options. Miles of beach front, ocean life, coral reefs, and bays for boating, fishing, and more.

Bavaro Beach, Dominican Republic

A favorite amongst Caribbean travelers, Bavaro Beach is located in Punta Cana. Stay at one of the popular resorts dotting the stretch of white sand. or visit a restaurant or bar that sits along its shores.
