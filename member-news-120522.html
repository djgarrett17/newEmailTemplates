Important Enhancements to Fare Bundles, Operational Changes, Effective December 14

Princess Plus and Princess Premier now offer even more value

Due to the growing demand of these fare bundles, Princess has enhanced both Princess Plus and Princess Premier with additional amenities, new premium indulgences and premium health and wellness offerings. As a result, the per day cost for each of these packages will increase nominally to your clients. The changes below are effective with new bookings made on or after Dec 14, 2022 and apply to voyages sailing on or after Feb 20, 2023*:

Princess Plus - Cost: Princess Plus will be priced at $60 per day per guest. Guests may still purchase the current package at the $50 per day price up until Dec 13, 2022. Value proposition: With the enhanced Princess Plus, your clients will enjoy $140 of value each day for just $60 per day – a 57% savings.

Princess Premier - Cost: Princess Premier will be priced at $80 per day per guest. Guests may still purchase the current package at the $75 per day price up until Dec 13, 2022. Value proposition: With the enhanced Princess Premier, your clients will enjoy $257 of value each day for just $80 per day – a 69% savings.

Crew appreciation - The daily Crew Appreciation charge is based on stateroom category, and effective Dec 14, 2022, will increase to: $16 per guest in Interior, Oceanview and Balcony (was $14.50), $17 per guest in Mini-Suite and Club Class (was $15.50), $18 per guest in Suites (was $16.50) for voyages sailing on or after Feb 20, 2023.

MedallionNet® - the best Wi-Fi at sea, effective with voyages sailing Feb 20, 2023 and beyond, will be priced as follows: $15 per day for single-device plan; $40 per day for four-device plan.
